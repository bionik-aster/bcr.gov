Our Vision

The Bench Communist Republic strives for total equality, social balance and absence of a centralised currency. The power of the State is only limited to public spaces, contrary to what the name "Communist" may suggest. We only hope for a better world, for all its people.

General Secretary's Welcome

Welcome! Welcome to the greatest thing humanity has ever seen since the birth of itself! We welcome all to our humble micronation. Though we may lack in experience, the ambition of the Bench Communist Republic will forever be my goal to uphold.
