Anthem of the Bench Communist Republic

Sung to the tune of the Soviet State Anthem

Verse 1:

O' Benchic Republic, for freedom still striving,

O' Bold, Crimson flag, waving clear in the wind!

For all of its people, the land that we toil,

Our banner will rise, unbowed through the storm!

Chorus

Great shall your glory be, Motherland bold and free!

Stronghold of Justice, eternal and grand!

O' Flag of our Republic! Guide us to victory!

Flag of the People, linked hand in hand!

Verse 2:

The sunlight of Freedom has beamed through the tempest,

And Irving has lighted the path with their Flame!

Through rain, storm and snow, our banner still rises,

So Comrades, fight forwards for Freedom and Fame!

Chorus

Great shall your glory be, Motherland bold and free!

Stronghold of Justice, eternal and grand!

O' Flag of our Republic! Guide us to victory!

Flag of the People, linked hand in hand!

Verse 3:

Our motto still bearing, our hearts ever Brighter,

For victory's in view, for our brethren we march!

The fate of the Future is sealed by our courage,

So rise, all ye working, and give it your all!

Chorus

Great shall your glory be, Motherland bold and free!

Stronghold of Justice, eternal and grand!

O' Flag of our Republic! Guide us to victory!

Flag of the People, linked hand in hand!
