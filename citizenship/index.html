Citizenship

Applying for citizenship

Citizenship of the Virtual Bench (see Government) can be requested through the Discord server, found in the footer below every page of this website. Citizenship of the Physical Bench however requires you to have witnessed it firsthand.

Border Guard

Maintained by the Bureau of Internal Security, the Border Guard is the primary decisive factor as to whether you are permitted into the nation or not. They, along with the General Secretary and other Bureau Secretaries, hold the final say in your citizenship.

Link to the portal here

Citizenship and Deportation

Depending on the severity of the crime, since the BCR cannot legally enforce capital nor corporal punishment, any severe enough crime will warrant either a blacklist or deportation, regardless of your citizenship status.

Honorifics

Honorific systems are strictly limited to administrative roles in the Communist Republic, disregarding all contribution-based honorifics apart from the Order of the Benchic Star (OBST), given for excellent service directly related to National Security.
